Included Features

  • Historical price fetch (custom period & interval)
  • Company fundamentals and quick info
  • Save / manage a simple portfolio
  • Interactive charts (Chart.js)
  • Simple analytics: SMA50, SMA200
  • Contact form (stores messages locally)
  • Multi-page layout (Home, Features, Portfolio, Analytics, About, Contact)
How it works (technical)

The backend uses yfinance to retrieve Ticker objects and historical data, converts them to Pandas DataFrames and serves JSON to the frontend. Caching is file-based to reduce repeated downloads.
